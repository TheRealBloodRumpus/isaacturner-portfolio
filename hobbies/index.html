Music

I'm a record and tape collector.

I mostly listen to crust-punk, anarcho-punk, d-beat, death metal and black metal with a little bit of electronica, industrial and noise thrown in.

I play guitar, bass, drums and modular synth.

I like explorer-style guitars.

I like Mesa-Boogie and Orange amps.

Board Games

I tend to like light to medium weight Euros, but I've been known to burn a whole day on Twilight Imperium or Star Wars Rebellion.

Mushroom Hunting

My wife Mika and I forage wild mushrooms. In the past I haven't been much of an outdoors type but something about hunting and identifying fungus really clicked for me. Just finding a mushroom I've never seen before is exciting. Even better when it's food.

Video Games

I mostly play indie games like Rift Wizard, Balatro, Post Void, Animal Well, Risk of Rain 2, Binding of Isaac, FTL and Into The Breach. I like old-school roguelikes with modern twists like Caves of Qud and Brogue.

I'm always stoked about whatever Hideo Kojima is working on.

I've got a soft spot for the old 16-bit console games like Super Mario World, Super Metroid, A Link to the Past, Sonic the Hedgehog, Toejam and Earl and Kid Chameleon.

I love the 8-bit era too... the Mario games on the NES, Mega Man, Battletoads, Bubble Bobble and Legend of Zelda.

I dig the old arcade machines like (fast) Ms. Pac-Man, Donkey Kong, Galaga, Centipede, 1943, Metal Slug and Street Fighter and I can play Ice Cold Beer for hours.
