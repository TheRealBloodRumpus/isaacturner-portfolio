Mushroom Hunting

My wife Mika and I forage wild mushrooms. In the past I haven't been much of an outdoors type but something about hunting and identifying fungus really clicked for me. Just finding a mushroom I've never seen before is exciting. Even better when it's food.

It's given me a much greater appreciation for just being out in the woods. Plus a lot of these things are delicious.
